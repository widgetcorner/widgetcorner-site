Play On Con Drivers

Privacy Policy

Effective: June 30, 2026

Summary: The app only sends data while you have broadcasting turned on. It sends your GPS location, direction, speed, the cart you picked at sign-in, and the name you typed at sign-in — and nothing else. No contacts, photos, microphone, camera, web history, or advertising IDs are touched. Stop broadcasting and the app stops sending.

Who this policy covers

This policy applies to the Play On Con Drivers mobile app (“the app”) for iOS and Android, published by Widget Corner LLC (“we,” “us”). The app is for volunteer drivers operating Play On Con shuttle carts.

What we send while you are broadcasting

After you sign in and tap Start broadcasting, the app sends the following to a Play On Con backend roughly every ten seconds:

  • Your device’s GPS location (latitude and longitude)
  • Direction of travel and speed
  • The cart you picked at sign-in (Cart 1, Cart 2, or Cart 3)
  • The name you typed at sign-in (can be a nickname)

That is the entire list. The data is used to display the shuttle’s real-time position to attendees inside the main Play On Con app.

What we never access

The app does not read or send any of the following:

  • Contacts
  • Photos or the photo library
  • Microphone or audio
  • Camera or video
  • Web or browser history
  • Advertising IDs (IDFA / GAID)
  • Health, fitness, or motion data outside of GPS-derived speed and heading
  • Files outside the app’s own storage

When broadcasting is off

When broadcasting is off — before you tap Start, after you tap Stop, or when the app is closed — the app sends nothing. It does not track your location in the background.

The name you type

The sign-in name does not have to be your real name. A nickname is fine. The name is sent alongside your cart so other volunteers and event organizers can tell which volunteer is driving which cart.

Permissions

The app requests location permission so it can read GPS while broadcasting. Location use is limited to broadcasting sessions. The app does not request any other sensitive permission.

How long the data is kept

Broadcasted positions are intended to be ephemeral and used for live display during the event. Widget Corner LLC does not operate a long-term archive of driver positions. Event-side retention is governed by Play On Con organizers.

Analytics and advertising

None. The app contains no analytics SDKs, no advertising SDKs, no crash reporters that send data off your device, and no tracking of any kind.

Accounts

The app has no account system, no login, and no password. Signing in for a shift is just picking a name and a cart.

Children’s privacy

The app is intended for adult and teen volunteer drivers operating shuttle carts at Play On Con. It is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change what the app sends, we will update this page and update the effective date above. Material changes will also be noted in the app’s next release notes.
