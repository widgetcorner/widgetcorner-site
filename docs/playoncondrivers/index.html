Play On Con Drivers

A volunteer companion app for Play On Con shuttle carts. Sign in, pick your cart, and broadcast your live location so attendees can see where the shuttles are.

About

Play On Con Drivers is a small purpose-built app for the volunteers who drive the shuttle carts during Play On Con. Sign in for your shift, pick the cart you’re driving, and tap to start broadcasting. While you’re broadcasting, your cart’s position appears in real time inside the main Play On Con app so attendees know when a ride is coming.

When you’re done driving, tap stop. The app does nothing in the background.

Features

  • Sign in with a name and cart. The name can be a nickname — it does not have to be your real name. Pick Cart 1, Cart 2, or Cart 3.
  • One-tap broadcasting. Tap Start broadcasting when you begin a shift. Tap Stop when you’re done.
  • Live shuttle location. While broadcasting, the app sends GPS, direction, and speed every ten seconds so attendees can see the cart move.
  • No background tracking. The app only sends location while broadcasting is on. Stop broadcasting and it stops sending.
  • Built for spotty signal. The app is designed for the kind of coverage you get on convention grounds, not downtown 5G.

Privacy

The app only sends data while you have broadcasting turned on. It sends GPS, direction, speed, your selected cart, and the name you typed at sign-in — and nothing else. No contacts, photos, microphone, camera, web history, or advertising IDs are touched.
