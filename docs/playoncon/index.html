Play On Con

The official companion app for Play On Con — full schedule, venue map, and reminders. Works offline. No account required.

About

Play On Con is a four-day tabletop and gaming convention at the Alabama 4-H Center in Columbiana, Alabama, featuring theater, RPGs, board games, video games, parties, and outdoor activities.

This app is built offline-first so attendees can navigate sessions and venues even where cell signal is spotty — cabins, the Lodge, or out on the grounds.

Features

  • Full four-day schedule. Every session across every venue. The view auto-opens to the current time slot.
  • My Schedule. Save sessions you want to attend.
  • Reminders. Local notifications at start time or a custom number of minutes ahead. No account, no push server.
  • Venue map with “you are here.” Color-coded pins for Stages, Gaming, Parties, Outdoors, and Stay & Eat. Location use is opt-in.
  • Tap to explore. See what’s happening at each venue. Jump from any session to its venue pin.
  • Session tags. Ages 21+, PG-13, Apprentice Track, Auditioned/Casted, Sensory Friendly, Sign-up at Open Gaming.
  • Discord link and sync status. Quick community access and a clear last-synced indicator.

Privacy

No account. No login. No ads. No tracking. Zero data collected. Location use is opt-in and stays on your device.
