Of Wand & Flame

Privacy Policy

Effective: June 30, 2026

Summary: Of Wand & Flame has no remote backend, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party SDKs. Your spells, notes, preferences, and Sign in with Apple credential live on your device only — in SwiftData and Keychain. Nothing is sent to us.

Who this policy covers

This policy applies to the Of Wand & Flame mobile app (“the app”) for iPhone and iPad, published by Widget Corner LLC (“we,” “us”). It does not cover any third-party services you might reach through links in the app.

Information stored on your device

The app stores the following on your device only:

  • Your Sign in with Apple credential — a stable, private user ID issued by Apple, plus the name and email Apple delivers to the app on first authorization. Apple may give you the option to hide your real email. The credential is kept in the iOS Keychain.
  • A record that you acknowledged the first-run disclaimer (which version, and when), tied to your credential.
  • Your authored content: written spells, saved/favorited spells, and grimoire notes — stored in SwiftData.
  • Lightweight preferences such as a display name — stored in UserDefaults.

All of this lives on your device. None of it is sent to Widget Corner LLC or to any third party.

What we collect

Nothing. The app does not transmit personal information to us. We do not operate a backend for this app, do not receive your credential or name or email, and do not see anything you write in the app.

Analytics, advertising, and tracking

None. The app contains no analytics SDKs, no advertising SDKs, no crash reporters that send data off your device, and no tracking technology of any kind.

Sign in with Apple

The app uses Apple’s Sign in with Apple service so you can have a private credential without creating a username or password. Your interaction with that service is governed by Apple’s privacy policy. We receive only what Apple hands the app on-device, and we do not forward it anywhere.

iCloud and device backups

If you have iCloud Backup or encrypted local device backups enabled in iOS, your app data may be included in those backups under your control. Widget Corner LLC does not access those backups.

Children’s privacy

The app is intended for adult practitioners and is not directed to children under 13. Because the app collects no information at all, no special handling is required.

Encryption

The app does not use non-exempt encryption.

Deleting your data

Because your data lives only on your device, uninstalling the app removes it. To revoke the Sign in with Apple credential, go to Settings → [your name] → Sign-In & Security → Sign in with Apple, select Of Wand & Flame, and choose Stop Using Apple ID.

Third-party links

The app may contain links to external resources. When you follow such a link, you are subject to that service’s own privacy policy. We are not responsible for the practices of third-party services.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change what the app collects or stores, we will update this page and update the effective date above. Material changes will also be noted in the app’s next release notes.
