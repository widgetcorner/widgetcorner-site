Of Wand & Flame

A personal companion for ritual, reflection, and seasonal practice — pairing Ursula Seren’s mentorship with everyday tools for spells, the moon, and the Wheel of the Year.

About

Of Wand & Flame is a SwiftUI app for witchcraft practitioners — a quiet, on-device companion built around eight sabbat arcs, the moon’s cycle, and your own evolving grimoire. Grounded in Ursula Seren’s voice, it’s meant to sit beside your practice, not get in its way.

The app is private by design: there is no remote backend, no analytics, no advertising. Everything you write stays on your device.

Features

  • Wheel of the Year hub. Eight sabbat arcs — history, lore, correspondences, and celebrate prompts to mark each turning of the year.
  • Moon phase tracker. Current-phase guidance tied to your practice — what each phase invites you to do.
  • Spells library. Read prebuilt spells, save your favorites, and write your own.
  • Grimoire / Book of Shadows. A private place for personal spells and notes, kept on your device.
  • Correspondences reference. Herbs, colors, and intents — a quick lookup for when you’re building a working.
  • About the practitioner. A section grounding the app in Ursula Seren’s voice and approach.
  • Sign in with Apple. A single, private credential — no password, no email signup, no third-party login.

Privacy

No remote backend. No analytics. No third-party SDKs. Your spells, notes, and preferences live on your device in SwiftData and Keychain — and stay there.
