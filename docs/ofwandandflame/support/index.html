Support

Help with the Of Wand & Flame app — common questions and how to get in touch.

Frequently asked

What does the app do?

It’s a personal companion for witchcraft practice: a Wheel of the Year hub, a moon phase tracker, a spells library, your own grimoire, and a correspondences reference. It’s designed to sit quietly beside your practice.

Why do I need to sign in with Apple?

Sign in with Apple is the only way to keep your grimoire tied to you across reinstalls without setting up a username and password. Apple gives the app a stable, private user ID. The app never sees your Apple ID password and never shares your information with anyone.

Will my spells and notes sync between my iPhone and iPad?

Right now everything is stored locally on the device where you wrote it. Cross-device sync isn’t available yet.

What’s the first-run disclaimer?

A short acknowledgement that the app is a personal practice companion — not medical, legal, financial, or psychological advice. We record that you acknowledged it (which version and when) so you don’t have to see it every launch.

How do I save a spell to my favorites?

Open a spell from the library and tap the favorite icon. Favorites appear at the top of the library for quick access.

How do I write my own spell?

Go to your grimoire and tap the add button. Give the spell a title, intent, and any notes or correspondences you want to record. It’s saved on your device immediately.

Can I export my grimoire?

Export isn’t available in the current phase. Since your notes live in SwiftData on your device, the safest backup today is an encrypted iCloud or iTunes/Finder device backup.

What iOS version do I need?

iOS 26 or later, on iPhone or iPad.

How do I report a bug or request a feature?

Email support@widgetcorner.com. Include your device model and iOS version if possible.
